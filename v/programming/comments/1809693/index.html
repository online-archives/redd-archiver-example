Microservices, shake it down baby.

1    21 Apr 2017 20:40 by u/roznak

I have been wondering about micro-services. The idea is that it will make your system more fault tolerant and faster.

But has anyone considered the fact that a hacker could bring the micro-services down by using a resonance hacking technique? By alternating attacks across domains and in time you could find the resonance frequency of that service and shake itself to destruction.

You try to find that sweet spot where connection/reconnection to micro-services starts to confuse the micro-service.
