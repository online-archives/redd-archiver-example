Bookmarklet for commenting on a website with dissenter.com

2    01 Mar 2019 04:54 by u/aGameCalledCountries

Must be logged into dissenter.com for the bookmarklet to work on a page:

javascript:(function() {
    window.open("https://dissenter.com/discussion/begin?url="+document.location.href, "_blank");
})();
