Showerthought: This whole industry is suffering from mythical man month syndrome

1    01 Sep 2016 14:42 by u/pholmq

The field of IT have grown exponentially, and there are simply not enough skilled people. It's pretty rare with even ten years of experience. If you've never heard of mythical man month syndrome (which you should if you're a developer) it's about how efficiency decrease instead of increase when you involve more people into an effort, because then the people with experience that are effective, have to put down their pen and learn the rookies and deal with the mistakes they make.
