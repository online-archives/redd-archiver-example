Tinder is a tool that creates potential partnerships between people with similar goals. Some partnerships never occur, some partnerships last 30 seconds and others last a lifetime.

In the same vein, a service or app, like Tinder, that lets me scroll through other like minded people with similar or varying skills to form my startup "dreamteam" could be handy. I'm not talking about looking for employees, contractors or investors to work for my startup. Rather, I'm looking for entrepreneurial, skilled and like minded people to form partnerships.

See, you have to form that dreamteam first, then create a prototype together and then finally look for investors, etc. So the possibility for bankruptcy or success comes after forming that dreamteam.

So case in point, cofounderslab seems to do the job.
