Programming creativity challenge: draw a ladder

2    01 Mar 2017 22:09 by u/roznak

In response to these stupid white board questions that interviews will ask: "Draw a ladder" Show us your creativity that will fail their written answer test:

  • Left line, right line then the sports in between.
  • Left T rotated 90 degrees, flip to right now repeat until the bottom.
  • Load image from file, then clone and flip until the bottom.
  • Draw left side, then clone flip to the right side
  • Draw one big rectangle the size of the ladder, then cut square holes in between the sports.

Your turn.... Impress us.
